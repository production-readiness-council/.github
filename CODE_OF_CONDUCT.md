# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

The Production Readiness Council needs a contribution environment that supports technical rigor, disagreement in good faith, and respectful review.

## Expected behavior

Participants are expected to:

- critique ideas, evidence, and process rather than people
- disclose relevant conflicts when participating in reviews or decisions
- keep claims evidence-based and clearly mark uncertainty
- avoid harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior
- respect boundaries around private security disclosures and unpublished review material

## Unacceptable behavior

The following behaviors are not acceptable:

- harassment or abusive conduct
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- knowingly misrepresenting evidence, authorship, review status, or outcomes
- sponsor pressure or attempts to influence technical conclusions outside the documented process
- retaliation against contributors for raising process or conduct concerns

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the maintainers or council contacts identified by the organization at the time of the report.

## Enforcement

Enforcement actions may include warning, removal from discussions, temporary restriction, or permanent removal from participation spaces, depending on severity and pattern.
